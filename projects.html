Leigh's Cycle Centre

This is an app I designed for a Bicycle business that is freely available on the App Store under 'Leigh's Cycle Centre'. It communicates with a database stored on a server through a RESTful webservice. It allows users to search available stock and allows for the promotion of special offers through push notifications and a special offers page. Find it on the App store

Equipped

Periscope

Periscope is a startup providing conference analytics using the iBeacon technology. I am building a generic modular conference app that can be easily edited to meet specific conference needs as well as the underlying iBeacon technology.

Tube Assist

Tube Assist is a project very close to my heart and it is an app that sets an alarm to go off when you arrive at your destination tube station, allowing you to sleep or read or whatever. The underground environment isn't very mobile conducive. In order to keep track of what station you are at I have tried various methods. I am currently using the station WIFI mac-addresses. The project has been put on hold while I finish other projects.
